Introduction

The India national cricket team, also known as Team India or the Men in Blue, represents India in international cricket.

History

India played its first Test match in 1932 and has since become one of the most successful teams in world cricket.

Achievements

India has won multiple ICC trophies, including the Cricket World Cup in 1983 and 2011, and the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007.

Famous Players

Legendary players like Sachin Tendulkar, Kapil Dev, MS Dhoni, and Virat Kohli have been part of the Indian cricket team.
